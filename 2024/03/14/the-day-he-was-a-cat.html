The Day He Was a Cat

In this captivating short story podcast episode that explores the whimsical misadventure of a young boy named Arthe. After a homework project takes an unexpected turn, Arthe finds himself in the paws of a dilemma—transformed into a cat by his own magic. With his newfound whiskers and tail, the quest to regain his human form begins. Can Arthe navigate the challenges of his feline form and return to humanity without the aid of magic?
This magical realism podcast is a perfect listen for fans of transformation tales, magical mishaps, and stories of resilience and ingenuity. Join Arthe as he embarks on a journey filled with challenges, adventures, and self-discovery, making The Day He Was a Cat a must-listen for those who love engaging in tales of magic, transformation, and the power of human spirit.
Voice Actor: Molly O'Donnell
